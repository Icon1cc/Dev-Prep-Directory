## ❓ FAQ

### Q: How much time per day?
**A:** 2.5-3.5 hours daily (solving) + 30 mins (review) = 3-4 hours total

### Q: Is this enough to get a job?
**A:** This program makes you interview-ready. You still need to apply, interview well, and potentially do system design interviews.

### Q: Can I skip days?
**A:** Better not to. The curriculum is sequential and builds on itself. If you miss a day, do it the next day.

### Q: What if I get stuck on a problem?
**A:** 1) Try for 25 mins, 2) Look at hint, 3) Look at approach, 4) Look at solution. Move on if stuck 60+ mins.

### Q: Which programming language?
**A:** Python is easiest and fastest for interviews. Java, C++, JavaScript also fine.

### Q: Can I do this in less than 45 days?
**A:** Not recommended. This pace is already fast. Quality over speed.

### Q: When should I start applying?
**A:** Week 7-8. By then you'll have solved 150+ problems and be ready.
